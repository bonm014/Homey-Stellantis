Do you want to be up to date with your car?
This application is for stellantis vehicle owners to allow to get insight of your car.
It allows you to read the percentage of you battery, as well the maintenance of your vehicle.

A trigger has been implemented to notify you of a new trip, exporting the consumption of the trip as well the distance and time taken.

Configuration:
Configure your app first by logging in and following the login steps
Use the PC/browser to login to you account and setup your connection.

Note:
As the connection for remote commands is different as reading the data, this is still in progress.
